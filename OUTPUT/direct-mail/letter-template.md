## Print Specifications

- **Paper:** 100gsm white or cream bond
- **Format:** A4, single-sided or double-sided fold
- **Envelope:** DL windowed envelope (address visible through window)
- **Postage:** 2nd class standard (cost-effective for bulk)
- **Branding:** Include Marley Moves logo at top, QR code near call-to-action

## Compliance Notes

- Ensure all data usage complies with GDPR and direct marketing regulations
- Include opt-out instructions if required by data source licensing
- Verify postal address accuracy before print to minimise waste
